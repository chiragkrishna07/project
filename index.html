Welcome to Awerks

Awerks Pvt Ltd is the company incorporated in India in 2022 in collaboration and partnership with RTI Technologies, Singapore. Our management team with collective experience of over 100 years in Electronics designs, manufacturing, QA, testing and customer support brings you a variety of products, parts and solutions for Electronics manufacturing and R&D.


Our fully automated state of art FRC cable assembly manufacturing plant is already operational at Bangalore which is the first fully automated plant in India.
