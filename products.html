High Quality IDC Flexible Ribbon Cables Fully Automated Cable Assembly Manufactured in India

An insulation-displacement contact (IDC), is designed to connect to the conductor(s) of an insulated cable by a connection process which selectively pierces the insulation of the cable to make contact with the cable conductor.
